# telecom-churn-business-analysis
# Telecom Customer Churn & Revenue Analysis

## Business Problem
Customer churn is one of the biggest drivers of revenue loss in the telecom industry. 
The goal of this project is to analyze customer behavior, identify churn risk factors, 
and provide data-driven recommendations to reduce churn and improve retention.

## Tools Used
- Python (Pandas, Matplotlib)
- SQL
- Excel (for validation and summary metrics)

## Objectives
1. Identify key drivers of customer churn
2. Analyze revenue impact of churn
3. Build a simple churn risk model
4. Provide strategic business recommendations

## Key Insights
- Month-to-month contracts had the highest churn rate
- Customers with higher monthly charges were more likely to churn
- Long-term contract customers generated more stable revenue

## Business Recommendations
- Incentivize long-term contracts with loyalty discounts
- Target high monthly charge customers with retention offers
- Develop churn risk monitoring dashboards
